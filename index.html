EU GDPR

Information and resources relating to the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

View & contribute to this resource on GitHub

The GDPR is a Regulation by which the European Commission intends to strengthen & unify data protection for individuals within the European Union and also address export of personal data outside the EU. The primary objectives of the GDPR are to give EU citizens back the control of their personal data and to simplify the regulatory environment for international business by unifying the regulation within the EU.

Timeline

  • 08/04/2016

    Adopted by the Council of the European Union

  • 16/04/2016

    Adoption by the European Parliament

  • 24/05/2016

    GDPR enters into force

  • 25/05/2018

    GDPR to be applied
